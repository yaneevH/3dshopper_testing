Industrial Fan with Lombardi Diesel Motor

  • Engine Type: Lombardi by Kohler Diesel
  • Engine Power: 35 hp
  • Function: Powers the Merris WX-5 Wood Waste Burner, generating the necessary airflow for the air curtain technology.
  • Operational Efficiency: Capable of processing up to 3 tons per hour (tph) of wood waste.
  • Weight: 22,500 lb
  • Portability: Transportable on trailers or flatbed trucks, and can be lifted using an excavator. It can be pulled around a job site by an excavator or loader.
  • Dimensions (Transport Size): 17'6" X 6' X 7'9"

Ladder for Easy Access and Fire Starting

  • Firebox Access: Ladder provides safe access to start a fire and add wood during the initial setup.
  • Operating Procedure: Start a fire in the chamber, add wood until the fire catches, then activate the fan to maintain combustion efficiency.
  • Extended Burning: Charcoal accumulates at the bottom, allowing extended burning when using a pit under the firebox.

High-Tech Refractory-Walled FireBoxes

  • Firebox Dimensions: 13' X 4'7" X 6', designed for high heat resistance and maximum combustion efficiency.
  • Heat Resistance: Built to endure extreme temperatures, ensuring durability and consistent performance.
  • Combustion Process: The firebox ensures complete combustion of wood waste, producing biochar as a byproduct.
  • Burning Capacity: Handles up to 3 tons per hour (tph) of wood waste efficiently.
  • Biochar Production: Post-burn biochar can be used as fertilizer, adding an eco-friendly advantage.

Air Diffuser to Contain Particle Emissions

  • Material: Thick metal pipe, designed to handle the high-temperature and rugged environment of the burn box.
  • Technology: Advanced air curtain technology creates a high-velocity airflow across the burn chamber.
  • Emission Control: The air curtain captures particulates and smoke, reburning them inside the chamber, ensuring minimal emissions.
  • Environmental Impact: Clean-burning and nearly smokeless operation, making it EPA Title V approved.
